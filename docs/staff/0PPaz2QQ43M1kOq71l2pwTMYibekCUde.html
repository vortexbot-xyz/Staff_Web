Vortex Staff Documentation

Please ensure you have read this information thoroughly as we will not accept any misconduct from staff members.

This documentation is private & confidential information. Do not share it to community members without approval from Senior Developers+

1. Staff activity

As a staff member, you are expected to meet a set quota every single week (every 7 days). You can find this quota listed below:

1. You must complete a minimum of 3 tickets
2. You must reach a total of 200 messages per week (excluding #public-support)
3. You must have a minimum of 50 messages inside of #public-support

2. Professionalism

Whilst within a staff position, you are expected to be professional when handling tickets, #public-support enquires or anything of similiarity. When talking within #staff-chat or #general you are not required to use grammar as long as when handling a situation you remain professional. We are not overly-strict on this rule as we would like a enjoyable experience as being a staff member whilst also remaining professional to ensure the expectations remain high from community members.


3. Chain of Command

You must anyways ensure that you refer people to the correct teams, do not directly ping @primxjxke.js because he's online. Go through other ranks first.

[ DEVELOPERS ]: Last place of contact. Do not contact developers (especially senior developer+) unless it is regarding something only we can handle.
[ COMMUNITY MANAGER ]: Community managers handle the staff team & manage giveaways, events & translation concerns. They will not handle anything that is not something you as support or moderation can handle.
[ SENIOR MODERATORS ]: Senior moderation members usually only handle major reports, or community reports. They will assist with moderation additonally but will not be the first place of contact regarding community member reports.
[ MODERATORS ]: Moderators usually handle with support team reports, they additonally handle community moderation concerns.
[ SENIOR SUPPORT ]: They are the second rank you obtain after joining the community staff team, they usually have more knowlage regarding the community & will directly handle anything that a support member cannot deal with.
[ SUPPORT ]: This is the first rank you obtain after joining our staff team, you'll be monitored closely & expected to handle concerns directly.
[ TRIAL SUPPORT ]: This rank is not an official staff-team rank, they are on trial & are expected to handle anything they are able to.

4. Command Permissions

The staff team within vortex have a private moderation & utilities bot available to anyone above the rank of Trial Support team. This bot has open commands to anyone that is Trial Support+ meaning you can use >ban as a support team member. Please read our command restrictions below as we do not encourage using commands without permission.

** If a command is not listed here, it is most likely allowed to be used by Trial Support+.

>modlogs - Trial Support+
>voidcase - Support+
>resetmodlogs - Senior Moderators+
>warn - Trial Support+
>mute - Support+
>kick - Moderators+
>ban - Senior Moderators++
